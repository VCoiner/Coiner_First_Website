Concussion

A clinical syndrome characterized by immediate and transient alteration in brain function, including alteration of mental status and level of consciousness, resulting from mechanical force or trauma.


Lateral Ankle Sprain

A strain is a stretching or tearing of muscle or tendon.


Laceration

A laceration is a wound that is produced by the tearing of soft body tissue. This type of wound is often irregular and jagged.


Abrasion

wearing, grinding, or rubbing away by friction. 2a : the rubbing or scraping of the surface layer of cells or tissue from an area of the skin or mucous membrane


Tendinitis

Inflammation of a tendon (the tissue by which muscle attaches to bone). Tendinitis most commonly occurs as a result of injury, such as to the tendons around the shoulder or elbow.


Fracture

A break in bone or cartilage. Although usually a result of trauma, a fracture can be the result of an acquired disease of bone, such as osteoporosis, or of abnormal formation of bone in a congenital disease of bone, such as osteogenesis imperfecta
